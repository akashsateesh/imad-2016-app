FARMER

Brief Description

The Farmer accesses the Website, searches for a commodities(machines,equipments etc) according to his requirements and use it on his field. Also, the farmer sells his crops at certain rate to the buyers. Initial Step-By-Step Description,before this use case can be initiated, the Farmer has already accessed the Website. 1.The Farmer searches for the required commodity in the market/warehouse. 2.Once the Farmer is satisfied based on the requirements the farmer buys the commodity at certain price. 3.The farmer can sell the crops at certain cost and may also specify the quantity available.

TRADER

Brief description

The Trader accesses the website, searches for crops according to his requirements and buys it. Initial Step-By-Step Description Before this use case can be initiated, the trader has already accessed the Website. 1.The trader chooses to search by crop name. 2. The system displays the choices to trader. 3.The trader chooses the best available based on his needs and buys it.
